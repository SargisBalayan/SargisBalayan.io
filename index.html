    WHAT WE DO

    E & A Insurance Center prides itself on providing personal service to its customers. We build long-standing relationships with our customers by simplifying the insurance shopping experience. Finding the right coverage can be a daunting task. We simplify the process by listening to your needs, shopping around for the best rates, and ultimately, placing you with the right coverage with the lowest cost. Also, one of our top priorities is to offer you the best insurance companies that we work with.

    We are based in Encino, CA

    With an extensive previous career in the banking sector, Edgar has been an insurance broker/owner at E & A Insurance Center since 2016. His professional career started in retail clothing then transitioned to banking where he became a Manager at Wells Fargo Bank in Woodland Hills, CA. Customer service has been at the heart of Edgar’s professional career. It was this dedication to acquiring and retaining customers for life that led Edgar to opening his own insurance company. With a profound knowledge of banking, the credit system and insurance, Edgar understands his customer’s perspective and offers solutions to their insurance needs that takes into account the Customer’s financial goals. He strongly feels “he is in business because of the loyalty of his customers.”
